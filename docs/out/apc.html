Q20 (or Q30 or Q40 etc)

Attribute

This is a quality score determined by phred which roughly corresponds to the probability of the call being incorrect. The probablility of a call being wrong is defined as:

for QN: E = 1 x 10 exp -(N/10).

(ie for Q20, the chance of error is 1 in 100)

Quality Length

Attribute

The Phred 20 length that is identified as the length of the contiguous region of an insert that is deemed high quality - see Quality Region. (This roughly correlates with the Q20 length).

Quality Vector

Attribute

The number of basepairs that are identified as vector within the 'quality region' of the insert.

Quality Region

Attribute

The region of the insert identified as high quality by phred. (This is based upon a general threshold of Q20, but the region may include base pairs of lower quality and may exclude base pairs greater than Q20).
