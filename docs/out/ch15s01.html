1. Inventory using the desktop and the scanner

Enable the user to audit the contents of a freezer. This will help ensure that the contents of any given rack in any equipment according to the system reflects what is actually in that rack and equipment

1.1. Features

  • Inventory tracking from the scanner or the desktop

  • Equipment(Freezer) auditing

  • Track time an inventory is started or completed

  • Keep statistics on the success rate of the audit

  • View past inventories and their statistics

1.2. Scanner Usage

Using the scanner:

  1. Scan in either an equipment

  2. Click on the Start Inventory link to start the inventory

  3. Scan in the shelves followed by the equipment to set the location of the racks using the inventory scan field

  4. Click on the update inventory button

  5. Scan in the racks followed by the shelf that the racks are located using the inventory scan field

  6. Click on the update inventory button

  7. Scan in the plates, solutions, or sources followed by the rack using the inventory scan field

  8. Click on the update inventory button

  9. Repeat steps 7-8 until all the items have been scanned into racks

  10. Click on Finish Inventory

1.3. Desktop

Using the desktop:

  • Go to the inventory home page

  • The inventory page will display past inventories and current inventories that are in progress based on the group of the user

  • The user can pick an inventory to start from the beginning

1.3.1. Start inventory from the beginning

  1. Scan in an equipment

  2. Click on the Start Inventory link to start the inventory

  3. Scan in the shelves followed by the equipment to set the location of the racks using the inventory scan field

  4. Click on the update inventory button

  5. Scan in the racks followed by the shelf that the racks are located using the inventory scan field

  6. Click on the update inventory button

  7. Scan in the plates, solutions, or sources followed by the rack using the inventory scan field

  8. Click on the update inventory button

  9. Repeat steps 7-8 until all the items have been scanned into racks

  10. Click on Finish Inventory

1.3.2. Choose an Inventory that is in progress

  1. Click on the Continue link from the list of inventories in progress

  2. The user can choose to:

    • view the original state of the equipment/rack before the inventory

    • view the current state of the equipment/rack (list of items in the racks/shelf/freezer

    • view the outstanding items that are supposed to be in the location

    • view the items in the location that are not supposed to be there

    • continue scanning in items

  3. Scan in the location designations

  4. Click on the update inventory button

  5. Scan in the plates, solutions, or sources followed by the rack using the inventory scan field

  6. Click on the update inventory button

  7. Repeat steps 5-6 until all the items have been scanned into racks

  8. Click on Finish Inventory

1.4. Scanning in items to the scan field

When doing inventory for an equipment, it is recommended to scan the racks and shelves into the appropriate spots.

  1. Scan in the target item

  2. Scan in the items that are the contents of the target item

  3. Click on the update inventory button

Example 15.1. Scanning shelves into equipment

  1. The target item (equipment) is prefilled

  2. Scan in the items that are the contents of the target item

  3. Scan rac100rac200rac300

  4. Click update inventory

Results in rac 100,200,and 300 moved to equipment 1

Example 15.2. Scanning items into a rack

  1. Scan rac4 into the target item field

  2. Scan sol1pla2src3 in the contents field

  3. Click update inventory

Results in sol1,pla2,and src3 moved to rac4
