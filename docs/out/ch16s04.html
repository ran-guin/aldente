4. DNA Fingerprinting and Sizing

Functionality specific to DNA fingerprinting is also supported within the alDente system, including automatic generation of thumbnails of TIFF images of gel runs. Advanced functionality is also available to perform special tasks such as picking out individual bands from gels and defining them as new samples.
